  • go to the bottom of the page, click the left button Get 30-Day Trial of Real-Time! just below the text

    MetaStock R/T

  • create account (i personally use one time generated mails via dns provider)

  • use credit card supplied by revolut (single use), whenever it asks you
  • add all indices to your trial account, so you can access broader range of data
